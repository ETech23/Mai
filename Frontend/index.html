About MAI

How the Mining Phase Works

Mai employs an AI-powered mining system, allowing users to earn tokens seamlessly through participation, engagement, and collaboration. This innovative approach ensures both fairness and scalability, rewarding users based on their activity and community growth contributions.

Key Aspects of Mai‚Äôs Mining System

  • Active Participation:

    Users are rewarded for remaining active during mining sessions. Mining operates on a session-based model, where users activate mining for a set duration.

  • Engagement with AI-Driven Activities:

    Activities such as interacting with the AI chatbot, completing tasks, or providing feedback contribute to token accumulation.

  • Collaboration via Referrals:

    Referring others to join Mai not only grows the community but also enhances your mining rate. Every successful referral increases the mining bonus by 5%.

Mining Rate Mechanism

The mining rate dynamically adjusts based on referrals, ensuring early adopters and active users gain fair rewards.

Key Components

  • Base Rate: 0.0005MAI per second
  • Referral Bonus: +5% per referred user

    • Mining Rate Formula

    \[ \begin{aligned} \text{Mining Rate} &= \\ \text{Base Rate} \times \left(1 + (\text{referralCount} \times 0.05)\right) \end{aligned} \]

    Example Calculations

    • 5 Referrals:
      \[ \begin{aligned} &0.0005 \times (1 + (5 \times 0.05)) \\ &= 0.0005 \times 1.25 \\ &= 0.000625 \end{aligned} \]
    • 10 Referrals:
      \[ \begin{aligned} &0.0005 \times (1 + (10 \times 0.05)) \\ &= 0.0005 \times 1.5 \\ &= 0.00075 \end{aligned} \]
    • Midpoint Check:
      \[ \begin{aligned} &\frac{0.0005 + 0.00075}{2} \\ &= \frac{0.00125}{2} \\ &= 0.000625 \end{aligned} \]

    Why It Seems Off

    The values appear inconsistent because the increase is linear, not exponential. A 5% bonus per referral scales linearly with the number of referrals. The math holds up, and the linear growth ensures consistency.

    This dynamic calculation ensures users are incentivized to expand the Mai ecosystem through referrals.
