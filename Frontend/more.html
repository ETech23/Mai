Privacy Policy

At MAI Project, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our platform.

We collect personal information, including your name, email address, and other details you provide when signing up or using our services.

We use your information to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, and enhance your experience on the platform.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or in the case of business transfers.

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Our platform may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
