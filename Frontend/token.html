How Mining Works on the MAI Platform

The MAI Platform introduces a unique mining mechanism designed to reward early adopters while ensuring a fair and sustainable distribution of MAI tokens. This article provides a detailed explanation of the mining process, including the base rate, referral bonuses, and rate adjustments based on user milestones.

Base Mining Rate

The mining process begins with a base rate of 0.0005 MAI tokens per mining action. This rate is fixed for the first 100,000 users on the platform. Each user earns tokens based on their mining activity, with the base rate serving as the starting point for all calculations.

Referral Bonuses

To incentivize user growth and community engagement, the MAI Platform offers a 5% referral bonus for each successful referral. This bonus is applied to the base rate, increasing the mining rate for users who actively invite others to join the platform.

The mining rate for a user with referrals can be calculated using the following formula:

\[ \begin{split} \text{Mining Rate} &= \text{Base Rate} \\ &\quad \times (1 + 0.05 \times \text{Number of Referrals}) \end{split} \]

Example:

If a user has 3 referrals, their mining rate would be:

\[ \begin{split} \text{Mining Rate} &= 0.0005 \\ &\quad \times (1 + 0.05 \times 3) \\ &= 0.0005 \times 1.15 \\ &= 0.000575 \, \text{MAI per mining action} \end{split} \]

Rate Adjustments Based on User Milestones

To ensure a fair distribution of tokens and align with the platform’s long-term sustainability, the mining rate is adjusted in a descending order as the number of users grows. These adjustments occur at specific user milestones:

  • First 100,000 Users: Base Rate: 0.0005 MAI per mining action.
  • 100,001 to 1,000,000 Users: Base Rate: 0.00025 MAI per mining action.
  • 1,000,001 to 5,000,000 Users: Base Rate: 0.000125 MAI per mining action.
  • Beyond 5,000,000 Users: The base rate will continue to adjust in a similar descending pattern until the total supply of 10 billion MAI tokens is reached.

Example:

If a user joins when the platform has 150,000 users, their base rate would be 0.00025 MAI per mining action. If they have 2 referrals, their mining rate would be:

\[ \begin{split} \text{Mining Rate} &= 0.00025 \\ &\quad \times (1 + 0.05 \times 2) \\ &= 0.00025 \times 1.10 \\ &= 0.000275 \, \text{MAI per mining action} \end{split} \]

Total Supply and Sustainability

The MAI Platform is designed with a total supply of 10 billion MAI tokens. The mining mechanism ensures that tokens are distributed gradually and fairly, with rate adjustments preventing rapid depletion of the supply. This approach balances early adopter rewards with long-term platform sustainability.

Key Takeaways

  • The base mining rate starts at 0.0005 MAI per mining action for the first 100,000 users.
  • Users can increase their mining rate by 5% per referral, calculated using the formula:
    \[ \begin{split} \text{Mining Rate} &= \text{Base Rate} \\ &\quad \times (1 + 0.05 \times \text{Number of Referrals}) \end{split} \]
  • The base rate adjusts in a descending order at user milestones (e.g., 100,000, 1,000,000, 5,000,000) to ensure fair distribution.
  • The total supply is capped at 10 billion MAI tokens, with the mining mechanism designed to sustain token distribution over time.
